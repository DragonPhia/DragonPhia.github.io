Dine on Campus

Looking for your ideal meal on UNCG campus? Here's a list of your fellow Spartan's favorites around campus! They include mouthwatering dishes that made it's way to the top of the Spartan's for a reason, as well as the price of these dishes are like no other! Be sure to check out your peer's favorties and expand your tastebuds! Bring yourself or a friend, and look forward to these amazing dishes brought to you by UNCG!

Spartan's Favorites

Don

Description: Simple, booth-lined Japanese restaurant specializing in hot entrees like donburi, teriyaki & ramen.
Address: 423 Tate St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Hours: Monday - Friday: 11 AM - 3:30 PM, 5 - 10 PM; Sunday: Closed

Recommendations:

Salmon Teriyaki: $11.95
Bibimbap $13.45
Spicy Ramen $10.95

Yum Yum Better Ice Cream

Description: Cash-only mainstay turning out ice cream & hot dogs since 1906 amid an old-school family-run vibe.
Address: 1219 Spring Garden St, Greensboro, NC 27403
Hours: Saturday, Monday: 10:30 AM - 5:30 PM; Tuesday - Friday: 10:30 AM - 9 PM; Sunday: Closed

Recommendations:

Chocolate Ice Cream: $2.25
All the way Hot dog: $1.80
Lemon Ice Cream: $2.25

Old Town Draught House

Description: Clubby pub vibe with a long list of beers on tap & menu of sandwiches, pizzas & other tavern fare.
Address: 1205 Spring Garden St, Greensboro, NC 274033
Hours: Open Everyday: 11 AM - 11 PM

Recommendations:

Wings(10): $10.00
Pretzel: $5.00
Chicken Philly: $10.00
